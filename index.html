Travel to Kalaw

It is a hill town in Shan State,Myanmar. The air is cool, the atmosphere is calm and the tree-lined streets still contain a smattering of colonial-era architecture

Places you should visit

  • Hnee Pagoda
  • Shwe Uu Min Pagoda
  • Christ the King Church

The things you will see

You will see a lot of flowers blossoming on the branches of the tree.

You can also watch the breathtaking and amazing view of the crops.
